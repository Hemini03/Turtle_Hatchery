Sri Lankan Turtle Population

There are about 250 species of turtles on Earth, where 7 of them live in the ocean and all the others on land or in freshwater ponds and marshes. Out of those 7 species of turtles who live in land, 5 visit the Sri Lankan shores to nest.

Interesting Turtle Facts

Turtles are one of the oldest reptiles in the world, older than snakes,crocodiles and aligators.Which date back to the times of dinosaurs. Most sea turtles nest at night, only one type of turtle nest at day time. It's easy to recognize turtles by their bony,cartilaginous shell, which act as a shield to protect themselves from their predators.

Why Are Turtles Important?

Turtles, mainly sea turtles are incredibly important for the eco-system because they are "keystone species". They also play an important part in planet's food web. They play a main role by maintaining the health of the world's oceans.
