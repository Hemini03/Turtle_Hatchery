Turtle Catergories

Turtle Catergories In Sri Lanka

Let's get to know about the 5 types of turtles that you can find in the Sri Lankan sea. They are as follows,

  1. Green Turtle
  2. Hawksbill Turtle
  3. Olive Ridley Turtle
  4. Loggerhead Turtle
  5. Leatherback Turtle

Lets get to know about these amazing creatures a little bit more !!

Green Turtle

Green Turtle, also know as Chelonia mydas scientifically, are the most common type of turtle in the Sri Lankan ocean. They are the largest species of hard-shelled turtles and also the second largest from the sea turtles. Green turtles live about 60 to 7o years. They live all over the world and also can be found off the costs of more than 140 countries.

Hawskbill Turtle

Hawskbill Turtle can be known as one of the smallest species of turtles. They have a life span of 30 to 50 years, and one of the most endangered turtle sepcies. Their primary source of food is coral reef sponges. Hawskbill have existed on this planet for the last 100 million years.

Olive Ridley Turtle

Olive Ridley Turtle is the most abundant turtle species in the planet. They can be found globally in the costal waters of at least 80 countries. They are believed to be lived as much as other sea turtles.

Loggerhead Turtle

Loggerhead Turtles are most common in Mediterranean, nesting on beaches from Greece and Turkey to Israel and Libya. They are the largest of all hard-shelled turtles. Majority of these turtles nest in the western rims of the Atlantic and Indian oceans.

Leatherback Turtle

Leatherback Turtle is the largest turtle of all the sea turtles. And also the oldest of all the sea turtles which go back to more than 150 million years. Their lifespan is about 45 to 50 years.
